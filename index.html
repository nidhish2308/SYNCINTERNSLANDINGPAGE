About us



At The Shoe House, we believe style shouldn't demand a huge investment of time or money. Style should be comfortable, accessible and easy to achieve. That's why our footwear feels as good as it looks, effortlessly taking you from the boardroom to the restaurant, to the park with your family. We strive to ensure your shopping experience is easy on your wallet, and your schedule, by delivering fashion and value to everyone in your household.

The Shoe House opened in 2020 and the chain has since grown to more than 115 locations across India. We are still exploring more locations around the country to gather broader audience.
